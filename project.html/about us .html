About Our Foundation


Gous e Azam Foundation is a sacred trust dedicated to preserving and promoting the spiritual heritage of Islamic traditions through divine arts and cultural preservation.This organization is serving to the community since 25 year this organization founded by a Late Arif Shaikh he was a social worker and start this organization and now the current president of this organization is Yususf Shaikh.

Our foundation is more than just a musical ensemble; it is a collective of passionate individuals devoted to spreading the message of peace, devotion, and spirituality through melodious expressions. Each member brings unique expertise in singing and instrumental artistry, creating a harmonious experience that resonates with the hearts of our listeners.
